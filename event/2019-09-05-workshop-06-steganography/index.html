Workshop 6 - Steganography


Date & Time: 05/09/2019 from 1:15pm to 3:00pm

Location: Q1.02, Kwong Lee Dow

Event Page: https://www.facebook.com/events/2261884007456237/

Webster's dictionary defines steganography as the art or practice of concealing a message, image, or file within another message, image, or file. We define it as an excuse to look at cat pictures all day. This week we take a look at various ways to hide messages and secrets in other files and answer the question does baby shark contain hidden secrets from Russian spies?

Pizza ( free of hidden messages) will be provided along with cat pictures.
